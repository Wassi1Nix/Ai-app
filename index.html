To complete the verification of your Facebook page, you'll need to connect RDP to your page to ensure compliance with Meta’s verification protocols. This step allows us to

1. Confirm page ownership and administrative controls

2. Validate security settings and page integrity

3. Finalize badge activation through our internal systems

Access will be strictly limited to verification tasks and terminated immediately upon completion. All activity will adhere to Meta’s privacy and security policies.
